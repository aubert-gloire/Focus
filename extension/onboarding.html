FOCUS

Your personal focus tracker

How it works

1
Describe your task

Tell FOCUS what you are working on, how long you plan to work, and paste the link to where you will be — e.g. your YouTube tutorial, Coursera course, or Perusall assignment.

2
Work normally

FOCUS runs quietly in the background, watching which tabs you visit and comparing them against your task.

3
Get redirected when you drift

If you open an off-task tab, FOCUS intercepts it and gives you 10 seconds to return — or override if it was intentional.

4
Review your session

After stopping, see your focus score, how many blocks fired, and export your logs any time.

Privacy — what we collect and where it stays

  • All data is stored locally on your device using browser storage. Nothing is sent to any server.
  • Tab titles and URLs are logged only during an active session you start.
  • You can export or delete your data any time from the popup.
  • FOCUS never reads page content, only tab titles and URLs.
