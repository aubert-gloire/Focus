FOCUS

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Short version: FOCUS stores all data locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to a server. We do not collect, share, or sell any personal information.

1. What FOCUS does

FOCUS is a browser extension that helps you stay on task during a work or study session. When you start a session, you tell FOCUS what you are working on, paste the link to where you will be working, and set an expected duration. FOCUS monitors which browser tabs you visit and redirects you back to your task if you open an unrelated site.

2. Data we collect

During an active session FOCUS records:

  • The task description you typed
  • The task URL you provided
  • The titles and URLs of tabs you visit during the session
  • The time spent on each tab (dwell time)
  • How many times the distraction block was triggered

FOCUS never reads the content of web pages — only the tab title and URL shown in the browser's address bar.

3. Where your data is stored

All session data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage (chrome.storage.local). This data never leaves your device. FOCUS has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

When you close your browser, the active session is cleared automatically. Each new session starts completely fresh.

4. Data sharing

We do not share, sell, transmit, or disclose your data to any third party — ever. There is no third-party analytics, advertising SDK, or tracking code in FOCUS.

5. Machine learning model

FOCUS uses a lightweight on-device machine learning model to decide whether a tab is related to your task. This model runs entirely inside your browser. No data is sent to any external service for classification.

The model was trained on synthetic (artificially generated) data and does not learn from your personal browsing history.

6. Permissions

FOCUS requests the following browser permissions:

  • tabs — to detect when you switch tabs
  • storage — to save your active session locally
  • notifications — to alert you when your session time is up
  • alarms — to check session duration in the background
  • scripting — to redirect distraction tabs to the block page

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit personal data.

7. Children's privacy

FOCUS does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The extension stores no personal information and has no account system.

8. Changes to this policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. Continued use of FOCUS after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
